FAQ

Common questions

Answers about bookings, pricing, support, and travel planning with Globe Go Travels.

Do you book flights?

Yes. We help with flight booking Canada for domestic and international travel, including one-way, round-trip, and multi-city trips.

Can you help with all-inclusive vacations?

Yes. We can help compare all-inclusive vacation packages for beach trips, family vacations, and holiday travel.

Do prices change?

Yes. Travel pricing can change based on availability, travel dates, and supplier inventory. We always recommend checking current quotes.

Can I request a custom quote?

Absolutely. Use the Request a Quote form to share your trip details, budget, destination, and special requests.

Do you help with cruises?

Yes. We can help with cruise booking Canada, cabin selection, and trip planning before and after your sailing.

Can you book travel for families or groups?

Yes. We support family travel, group travel, couples, solo travellers, and larger custom itineraries.

How do I pay?

Payment methods can be discussed during the booking process. The payment page is prepared for secure payment instructions, but no card details are collected on this static website.

Do you provide support after booking?

Yes. We aim to support clients before and after booking so they have help if plans change or questions come up.

Can I look up my PNR online?

The PNR lookup page is ready for a future airline, GDS, NDC, or booking engine integration. It does not retrieve live reservation records yet.

What IATA policies should I review?

Before payment, review the IATA-aligned policy page for passenger names, ticketing, documents, baggage, dangerous goods, refunds, payments, privacy, and supplier terms.
