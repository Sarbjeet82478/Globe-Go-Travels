Recommended automation flow

  • 1. Quote submitted: form sends email to Globe Go Travels and confirmation to customer.
  • 2. CRM lead created: customer details, trip type, budget, and dates are saved.
  • 3. Agent follow-up: lead moves through contacted, quoted, booked, paid, and completed statuses.
  • 4. Payment link sent: customer pays through a PCI-compliant processor.
  • 5. Confirmation issued: invoice, supplier references, airline ticket details, and legal notices are sent.

Important implementation note

Do not collect credit card numbers directly on this static website. Use a compliant payment provider and keep passport, date of birth, and payment details out of local browser storage unless a secure backend is added.
